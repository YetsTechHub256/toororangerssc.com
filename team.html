ABOUT CLUB

Tooro Rangers Sports Club is a limited Entity dedicated to helping soccer players of different levels reach their highest potential. We are a grassroots Sports club and run a variety of soccer programs. With our Pillars as our foundation, the TORASC also Embraces NET BALL a reason for owning a Netball Club.
